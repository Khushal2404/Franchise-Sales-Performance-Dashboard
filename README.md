# 📊 Franchise Sales Performance Dashboard

### 📈 A CRM Lead Analysis Case Study

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Business Analyst case study based on an anonymized CRM dataset containing **1,778 leads** managed by a franchise sales executive.

The objective was to clean and standardize raw CRM data, engineer business KPIs, analyze lead conversion and sales performance, and develop an executive dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making.

The final solution demonstrates how operational CRM data can be transformed into meaningful business insights through structured analysis, KPI engineering, and executive reporting using Microsoft Excel.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The CRM dataset contained **1,778 lead records** managed by a franchise sales executive. While the data captured valuable information such as lead status, package category, investment amount, revenue, and sales timelines, it was maintained in a raw operational format.

This made it difficult to evaluate sales performance, monitor lead conversion, identify regional trends, and measure sales efficiency through meaningful KPIs.

The objective of this case study was to transform raw CRM data into an executive-level dashboard capable of supporting data-driven decision-making through structured analysis and business reporting.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Clean and standardize raw CRM lead data for analysis.
- Create meaningful business KPIs from operational sales data.

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview

The project is based on an anonymized CRM dataset containing **1,778 lead records** managed by a franchise sales executive.

The dataset represents the complete sales journey of prospective franchise customers, including lead generation, customer information, package selection, revenue, sales status, and closure timelines.

The available data enabled analysis across multiple business dimensions, including:

- Lead Conversion
- Revenue Performance
- Zone-wise Sales
- Package-wise Performance
- Sales Cycle Efficiency

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

Before analysis, the CRM dataset was cleaned and standardized to improve data quality and reporting accuracy.

The following preprocessing activities were performed:

- Removed duplicate and unnecessary records.
- Standardized lead status values for consistency.
- Corrected inconsistent text formatting.
- Verified revenue and investment values.
- Created a **Days to Closure** metric using lead and closure dates.
- Categorized package types for comparative analysis.
- Validated missing and incomplete records wherever applicable.
- Prepared the dataset for Pivot Table and dashboard reporting.

---

## 📊 KPI Engineering

The dashboard was designed around key business metrics that provide a quick overview of sales performance and operational efficiency.

The following KPIs were developed:

- 📞 **Total Leads** – Tracks the overall lead volume captured in the CRM.
- 📈 **Conversion Rate** – Measures the percentage of leads converted into successful sales.
- 💰 **Revenue Generated** – Represents the total revenue generated from converted leads.
- 🚀 **Pipeline Value** – Estimates the potential revenue from active sales opportunities.
- 📉 **Lost Opportunity** – Highlights the revenue lost from unconverted or rejected leads.
- ⏱️ **Average Days to Closure** – Measures the average time required to convert a lead into a customer.

---

---

## 💡 Executive Insights

The dashboard highlighted several key business findings:

- Generated **1,778 leads** with an overall **conversion rate of 2.53%**.
- **West Zone** contributed the highest franchise revenue.
- **Starter Package** generated the highest total revenue.
- **Growth Package** achieved the fastest average closure time (**6.5 days**).
- **June** recorded the highest monthly franchise conversions.

- ---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were identified:

- Improve lead qualification to increase the current conversion rate.
- Replicate West Zone's sales strategy across underperforming regions.
- Reduce Starter Package closure time through structured sales follow-ups.
- Increase marketing efforts before high-performing months like June.
- Monitor pipeline opportunities regularly to improve conversion into successful sales.
